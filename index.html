Profile

Hey there! I'm Felix, a Head of Engineering with a talent for leading tech teams to greatness. Over the past decade, I've been building and scaling software platforms that users love, all while building teams and growing individuals.

I have the on the boots experience to not let dogma get in the way of getting stuff done. I like agile, sprints can be useful, kanban is cool, but all are just tools to help a team deliver reliable features with reasonable estimates, facilitating communication, and limiting burn out.

I've had the privilege of guiding global engineering teams, turning complex problems into elegant solutions, and ensuring our tech not only meets but exceeds business goals. At the heart of it, I'm all about fostering a culture where innovation, open communication, and a bit of fun lead to extraordinary outcomes. Whether it's enhancing user experiences or driving growth, I'm passionate about leveraging technology to make a real impact.
