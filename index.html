About Me

I'm a seasoned engineering leader. I enjoy building high-performing teams and scalable, robust software. I program in Scala, Typescript, and Python, with a deep appreciation for type safety, observability, and comprehensive testing.

Outside of my professional life, I'm passionate about improving democratic systems. As the former chair of the Center for Election Science, I've dedicated years to advocating for better voting methods.

Want to chat? Happy to hop on a call, or better, let's get a coffee in London. I ask for a $50 donation to the Center for Election Science for a 30 minute chat. I'm available to speak on social choice theory, and for engineering management mentorship.
